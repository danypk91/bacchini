In collaboration with theoretical physicists, we explore the connections between string theory, holography, and black hole physics. This interdisciplinary line of research investigates how insights from high-energy theory can inform our understanding of plasma processes near black holes, and conversely, how astrophysical observations may constrain theoretical models of quantum gravity. Topics include the fuzzball paradigm, black hole information paradox, and the interplay between gauge/gravity duality and magnetospheric plasma dynamics.
