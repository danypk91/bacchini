State-of-the-art plasma simulations require cutting-edge computational resources and efficient algorithms. Our group is actively involved in the development of scalable, GPU-accelerated particle-in-cell codes designed for leadership-class supercomputers. We work on performance optimization through MPI+OpenMP hybrid parallelism, GPU porting with CUDA and HIP, and novel data structures for particle management. Our codes are deployed on major European and international HPC facilities including LUMI, MeluXina, and VSC clusters.
